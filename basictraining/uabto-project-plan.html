CAAS Requirements Analysis

Key UABTO Requirements from AC 101-3-1(4):

Section 4.2 - Application Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Training syllabus and curriculum
  • Organizational structure
  • Personnel qualifications
  • Training facilities description
  • Quality assurance procedures
  • Record keeping procedures
  • Training materials and resources

Section 4.3 - Accountable Manager Requirements

  • Appropriate experience and qualifications
  • Ultimate responsibility for training organization
  • Authority to ensure compliance

Section 4.4 - Training Program Requirements

  • Covers all required learning outcomes
  • Appropriate assessment methods
  • Minimum training hours met (2+ hours per module)
  • Competency-based approach
