Our Organizational Structure

A lean and agile entity designed for compliance and excellence in unmanned aircraft training.

Core Philosophy

The Dronefly.sg Unmanned Aircraft Basic Training Organisation (UABTO) is structured as a lean and agile entity, led by a single, highly qualified individual who serves as the central point of accountability.

While the day-to-day operations are managed by the founder, the organization is built on a network of trusted, on-demand experts. This model allows us to maintain the highest standards of quality and compliance by engaging specialized professionals for key functions as needed, ensuring sustainability and excellence without the overhead of a large permanent staff.

Demonstrating Compliance & Professionalism

This "solopreneur-plus-experts" model is designed to meet and exceed the expectations of CAAS for a professional training organization:

Clear Accountability

By defining a single Accountable Manager, we establish a clear point of contact who is legally and financially responsible for the organization.

Commitment to Quality

We have a formal plan to maintain the quality and currency of our curriculum by bringing in external subject matter experts for periodic reviews.

Robust Technical Oversight

The security and reliability of our online platform are managed by dedicated technical professionals, ensuring compliance with PDPA and IM8 standards.

Independent Auditing

By planning for an annual external audit, we show a proactive commitment to quality assurance and continuous improvement.
