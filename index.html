The Essentials

Key Safety Regulations

These rules apply to all drone operators, regardless of weight or purpose. Following them ensures the safety of everyone.

✅

Maintain Visual Line-of-Sight

Always keep your drone where you can see it with your own eyes, not just on a screen.

❌

Stay Below 200ft (60m)

Do not fly higher than 200 feet above mean sea level unless you have a specific permit.

❌

Avoid No-Fly Zones

Do not fly within 5km of an airport or airbase. Always check the OneMap app before flying.

✅

Respect Privacy

Do not fly over private property or take photos/videos of people without their consent.

❌

Don't Fly Over Crowds

Avoid flying over groups of people, moving vehicles, or during bad weather.

❌

No Prohibited Actions

Never drop items from your drone or use it to carry hazardous materials.
